Account & Access Management

This page provides general information on how users may manage their connection, access settings, and any app-related permissions associated with their Facebook account.

Managing App Access Through Facebook

Users who wish to adjust or remove access granted to an app through Facebook Login can do so directly from their Facebook account settings. Removing an app from Facebook will revoke its permissions and disconnect it from the user’s profile.

  1. Open Facebook on mobile or desktop.
  2. Go to Settings & Privacy → Settings.
  3. Select Apps and Websites.
  4. Locate the app you want to manage.
  5. Choose Remove if you want to terminate access.
